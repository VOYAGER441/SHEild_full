Core Safety Features

Advanced protection tools at your fingertips

SOS Emergency Button

Instant alert system that notifies your emergency contacts with your location. With just one tap, your selected contacts receive an SMS with your current location and a distress signal.

Fake Call Feature

Create a fake call to escape uncomfortable situations discreetly. Schedule calls in advance or trigger them instantly with customizable caller names and ringtone.

Real-time Location Sharing

Share your real-time location with trusted contacts for added safety. Set time limits for sharing and receive notifications when your location is being tracked.

SHEild AI Assistant

Get personalized safety recommendations and support from our AI assistant. Receive contextual advice based on your location, time, and situation.

Community & Support Features

Connect with others and access resources

Community Support

Connect with other users in a safe community platform to share experiences, seek advice, and offer support. Moderated discussions ensure a respectful environment.

Offline Maps

Access maps without internet connection for reliable navigation anywhere. Download maps for specific regions and access them even in areas with poor connectivity.

Security & Privacy

Your safety and privacy are our top priorities.

SHEild employs industry-standard encryption to protect your data. All communications between your device and our servers are encrypted using TLS 1.3, ensuring that your personal information remains secure.

We never sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your location data is only shared with your explicit permission and only with your designated emergency contacts.

Our servers are hosted in secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring and physical security measures.
