General Questions

What is SHEild and how does it work? ▼

SHEild is a comprehensive women's safety application designed to provide personal security features and community support. The app offers several key features including:

  • SOS Emergency Button: Sends instant alerts to your emergency contacts with your location
  • Fake Call Feature: Creates realistic fake calls to help you exit uncomfortable situations
  • Location Sharing: Share your real-time location with trusted contacts
  • Community Support: Connect with other users in a moderated, safe community platform
  • SHEild AI Assistant: Get personalized safety recommendations based on your situation
  • Offline Maps: Access maps without internet connection for reliable navigation

The app works by combining your smartphone's capabilities with our secure servers to provide these safety features. All data is encrypted and protected according to our Privacy Policy.

Is SHEild free to use? ▼

Yes, SHEild is completely free to download and use. We believe that personal safety should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation. All core features of the app are available at no cost.

We may introduce premium features in the future, but we will always maintain a robust set of free safety features. Any paid features will be clearly marked, and you'll never be required to pay to access basic safety functionality.

Which platforms does SHEild support? ▼

SHEild is currently available for:

  • Android: Available on the Google Play Store for devices running Android 7.0 (Nougat) or higher
  • iOS: Available on the App Store for devices running iOS 12.0 or higher

We recommend keeping your device's operating system updated to ensure compatibility with the latest version of SHEild. The app requires certain permissions to function properly, such as location access for emergency features and contacts access to notify your emergency contacts.

How is my privacy protected? ▼

Your privacy and security are our top priorities. We implement several measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All communications between your device and our servers are encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect information necessary for the app to function
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Location Control: Your location data is only shared with your explicit permission and only with your designated emergency contacts
  • Secure Storage: Our servers are hosted in secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring

For more detailed information, please review our Privacy Policy.

Safety Features

How does the SOS feature work? ▼

The SOS feature is designed for emergency situations and works as follows:

  1. Activation: Press and hold the SOS button (usually accessible from your phone's lock screen or notification panel)
  2. Countdown: A 3-second countdown begins to give you a chance to cancel if pressed accidentally
  3. Alert Dispatch: If not canceled, the app sends an SMS with your location to your designated emergency contacts
  4. Live Tracking: Your location is continuously shared with your contacts until you deactivate the feature
  5. Audio Recording: (Optional) The app can record audio during the emergency for evidence

It's important to note that the SOS feature requires proper setup of emergency contacts and location permissions to function correctly. We recommend testing this feature in a safe environment to ensure it works as expected.

Can I customize the fake call feature? ▼

Yes, the fake call feature is highly customizable:

  • Caller Identity: Choose from preset contacts or create custom caller profiles
  • Ringtone: Select from various ringtone options or use your default ringtone
  • Call Duration: Set how long the call should last
  • Scheduling: Schedule calls in advance for specific dates and times
  • Conversation Script: Customize what the caller says during the conversation

The fake call feature is designed to be as realistic as possible to help you exit uncomfortable situations. You can trigger a call instantly or set it up in advance for situations you anticipate might be challenging.

How accurate is the location sharing feature? ▼

Our location sharing feature utilizes GPS, Wi-Fi, and cellular data to determine your position with high accuracy:

  • GPS Accuracy: Typically accurate to within 5 meters in open areas
  • Urban Areas: May be slightly less accurate (10-20 meters) due to buildings and obstacles
  • Indoor Locations: Accuracy varies depending on building construction and available Wi-Fi networks

To ensure the best accuracy:

  • Keep GPS enabled on your device
  • Allow the app to access your location in the background
  • Ensure you have a clear view of the sky when possible

    • Please note that location accuracy depends on your device's hardware and network conditions. In emergency situations, even approximate location information can be valuable to your contacts.

Technical Support

What should I do if the app isn't working properly? ▼

If you're experiencing issues with the app, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Restart the App: Close the app completely and reopen it
  2. Check Permissions: Ensure all required permissions (location, contacts, notifications) are granted
  3. Update the App: Make sure you're using the latest version from the app store
  4. Restart Your Device: Sometimes a simple restart can resolve issues
  5. Clear Cache: Clear the app's cache through your device's settings
  6. Reinstall: As a last resort, uninstall and reinstall the app

If these steps don't resolve the issue, please contact our support team at support@shieldapp.com with details about the problem, including your device model, operating system version, and app version.

Why am I not receiving notifications from the app? ▼

If you're not receiving notifications from SHEild, check these settings:

  1. In-App Notifications: Ensure notifications are enabled within the app settings
  2. Device Settings: Check your phone's notification settings for SHEild and ensure notifications are allowed
  3. Battery Optimization: Some devices have aggressive battery savers that can restrict background activity. Add SHEild to the whitelist of apps allowed to run in the background
  4. Do Not Disturb: Ensure Do Not Disturb mode isn't blocking app notifications
  5. Network Connectivity: Poor network connection can delay notifications

For emergency features like SOS alerts, we recommend testing the notification system to ensure it works properly. Critical safety notifications are designed to bypass most Do Not Disturb settings, but this can vary by device manufacturer.

How much battery does SHEild consume? ▼

We've optimized SHEild to minimize battery consumption while maintaining functionality:

  • Background Operation: The app uses minimal background processes to preserve battery life
  • Location Services: Location tracking is only active when needed (during emergencies or when sharing location)
  • Push Notifications: We use efficient push notification services that don't require constant polling

Typical battery usage:

  • Normal Use: 1-3% battery per day with moderate usage
  • Active Location Sharing: 5-10% battery per hour during continuous sharing
  • Emergency Mode: 3-5% battery per hour during active emergency alerts

You can reduce battery consumption by adjusting app settings, such as reducing location accuracy when not in immediate danger or limiting background app refresh.

Account & Security

How do I reset my password? ▼

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it using these steps:

  1. On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password"
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account
  3. Check your email for a password reset link (check spam/junk folders if not in inbox)
  4. Click the reset link and enter your new password
  5. Confirm your new password and log in

Password requirements:

  • At least 8 characters long
  • Include at least one uppercase letter
  • Include at least one lowercase letter
  • Include at least one number
  • Include at least one special character

For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, please contact our support team.

What happens if I lose my phone? ▼

If your phone with SHEild installed is lost or stolen:

  1. Remote Logout: Log in to your account from another device and remotely log out the lost phone
  2. Change Password: Immediately change your account password to prevent unauthorized access
  3. Notify Contacts: Inform your emergency contacts that they may receive false alerts from your lost device
  4. Disable Features: Temporarily disable location sharing and other features until you recover or replace your device
  5. Report to Authorities: File a report with local authorities if the phone was stolen

SHEild does not store sensitive personal data on your device, but we recommend taking these precautions to protect your account. The app does not have remote wipe capabilities, so it's important to secure your account through the steps above.

If you had location sharing active, your contacts will be notified that you've logged out of the app, which may help them understand that any subsequent location updates are not from you.
